Today we're launching **DuneUI** to the world. It's been a labor of love, and we couldn't be more excited to share it.

DuneUI started as a personal collection of components we kept reusing across projects. We cleaned them up, documented them, and decided to give them away for free. No account required, no limitations — just copy and build.

## What's included

- **20+ hand-crafted UI components** organized by category
- Component preview with live HTML source and one-click copy
- Fully **responsive** — every component works beautifully from mobile to desktop
- Clean, semantic HTML built on TailwindCSS
- Free for personal and commercial use

We're just getting started. More components, templates, and tooling are coming soon.
