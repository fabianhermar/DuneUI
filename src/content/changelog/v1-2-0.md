We heard you. The most requested category by far was marketing sections, and today we deliver. This update adds 6 new component categories purpose-built for landing pages and marketing sites.

## What's new

- **Hero Sections** — 8 variants including split layout, centered, with image, with video background
- **Pricing Sections** — with monthly/yearly billing toggle and feature comparison table
- **Testimonials** — avatar support, star ratings, carousel and grid variants
- **Newsletter Sections** — inline and card layouts with form integration
- **Content Sections** — for articles, changelogs, and editorial layouts
- **FAQ Sections** — accordion and two-column variants

## Improvements

- Smoother transition animations on interactive component previews
- All new components include dark mode class variants

## Bug fixes

- Resolved font-loading flash on component preview pages caused by FOIT
